Factorial Calculator

Calculate the factorial of a number using the for loop in python:

Code:
                        num=int(input("enter a number: "))
                        fact=1
                        for i in range (1,num+1):
                            fact=fact*i
                        print("The factoraial of {} is".format(num),fact)
Output:
enter a number: 4
The factoraial of 4 is 24
